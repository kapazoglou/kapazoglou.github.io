I’ve explored data visualization with D3.js and generative design through various experiments and side projects, pushing the boundaries of digital interaction. With D3.js, I’ve built interactive, scalable visualizations that simplify complex data, making information more accessible and actionable. My generative design projects use algorithms to create adaptive, evolving visual systems, enhancing engagement and customization.

> Experiments in AI assisted, algorithmic and generative design: turning data structures into visual forms.

### AI-Assisted

This website has been built as an experiment in agentic AI assisted design. I use Cursor, Figma Make and Claude Design to implement designs that I create in Figma in a workflow very similar to collaborating with a team of engineers. I find that prompt engineering for agents and handover readiness for engineers are very closely related and I have been generating results that are similarly close to the user stories/prompts I create.

{% include figma-embed-overlay.html embed_url="/romino" iframe_scale="0.8" preview_src="/assets/images/interactive-previews/romino.png" label="Open interactive demo: romino" hide_figma_logo=true cta_line1="Interactive" cta_line2="demo" %}

To further improve my skills, I am currently designing a WIP MVP of a game that has been on my mind for a very long time that combines the structures of playing cards and dice in unique new ways. Being able to rapidly prototype new ideas has made me a better game designer and has radically sped up my process.

### Generative

I've also explored data visualization with D3.js and generative design through various experiments and side projects, pushing the boundaries of digital interaction. With D3.js, I built interactive, scalable visualizations that simplify complex data, making information more accessible and actionable. My generative design projects use algorithms to create adaptive, evolving visual systems.
